# Compliance Assumptions

Privacy Guardian is not currently a compliance-certified product. It is an MVP designed with compliance principles in mind.

## GDPR Assumptions

- The product supports data subject rights workflows.
- Data minimisation is a core design requirement.
- Users should understand what data is stored and why.
- Retention and deletion rules must be implemented before production use.

## ISO Assumptions

- ISO/IEC 27001 informs security governance and risk management.
- ISO/IEC 27002 informs secure development, logging, and information protection controls.
- ISO/IEC 27701 informs privacy information management and documentation.

## Required Future Work

- DPIA-style review.
- Privacy notice.
- Data retention schedule.
- Incident response process.
- Vendor and subprocessor review before external integrations.
