# Product Scope

Privacy Guardian is a B2C privacy SaaS MVP that helps users identify organisations that may hold their personal data, understand privacy risk, and prepare GDPR-style data rights requests.

## MVP In Scope

- Health-checkable FastAPI backend.
- Demo-safe detected company workflow.
- Demo-safe risk score workflow.
- Controlled privacy request creation and status tracking.
- Structured audit event model and service.
- Privacy-safe SQLAlchemy models and migrations.
- Static frontend dashboard prototype.

## Out Of Scope For Now

- OAuth and authentication flows.
- External email APIs.
- AI features.
- Raw email body storage.
- Attachment storage.
- Automated sending of real requests.
- Production deployment.

## Product Principle

The MVP should prove the privacy-safe workflow before adding integrations. The product should be useful because it helps users act on privacy rights while reducing the chance that the platform itself becomes a new privacy risk.
